In this module, you'll learn the critical skill of effective prompting - the core competency that enables powerful AI interactions. We'll break down how Large Language Models (LLMs) actually work, explore prompt engineering techniques, and provide hands-on exercises to develop your prompting skills.

## Key Learning Objectives

- Understand the fundamental mechanics of how LLMs generate text
- Master the anatomy of a well-structured prompt
- Learn advanced prompting techniques like chain-of-thought and few-shot learning
- Develop strategies to improve AI reasoning and output quality
