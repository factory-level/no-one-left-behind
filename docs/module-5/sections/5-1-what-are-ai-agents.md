# 5.1 What Are AI Agents?

## Defining AI Agents
- Autonomous software entities capable of independent decision-making
- Goal-oriented systems that can perceive, reason, and act
- Evolution from static tools to dynamic problem-solvers

## Key Characteristics
- Autonomy: Self-directed operation
- Reactivity: Responding to environmental changes
- Proactivity: Taking initiative towards goals
- Social capability: Potential for interaction with other agents
